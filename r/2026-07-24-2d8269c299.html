Top 3
1

Cagan: The AI Productivity Paradox (Build to Learn vs Build to Earn)

New SVPG essay argues AI accelerates delivery but not outcomes — 89% of execs say AI made work faster, only 6% can name ROI. Root cause: teams use AI to speed up project-model delivery instead of deciding what's worth building. Cagan's fix is to separate 'Build to Learn' (discovery) from 'Build to Earn' (delivery).
⚡ For you: Use this to pressure-test your 10x HIH bets: before pointing AI at faster shipping, ask whether it's resolving the least-settled risk (value/usability), not just producing more code. Steal the Build-to-Learn / Build-to-Earn split as a gate in your mentor→PRD workflow so discovery spend is explicitly separated from delivery spend.
